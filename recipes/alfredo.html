Alfredo

Description

A pasta with a creamy garlic parmesan sauce

Ingredients

  • Pasta of your choice
  • Garlic
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Parmesan

Recipe

  1. boil pasta in salted water and set aside
  2. Melt butter in pan and roast garlic off on medium low heat until garlic is translucent
  3. Toss pasta in with butter and garlic
  4. Add milk and cover, let milk reduce
  5. Once milk reduce by about half add a healthy amount of grated parmesan
